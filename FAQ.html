Payment

Get help with payment

When is payment taken for my order?

–

Payment is taken during checkout. The order number on the confirmation screen means payment succeeded.

How do I pay for my order?

+

You can pay using cards, UPI, wallets, or net banking during checkout.

What should I do if I'm having trouble placing an order?

+

Try refreshing, switching browsers, or contact support if the issue persists.

Which license do I need for an end product that is only accessible to paying users?

+

You'll need an extended license for that scenario.

Does my subscription automatically renew?

+

Yes, it renews automatically unless canceled before the due date.
