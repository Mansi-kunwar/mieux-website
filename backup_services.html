Backup As a service (BaaS)

In today’s digital-first world, data protection is critical to business continuity. Mieux Technologies offers Backup as a Service (BaaS), a cloud-based solution designed to safeguard your enterprise data without the need for on-premise hardware or complex infrastructure. Our BaaS ensures automated, secure, and reliable backup and recovery, leveraging industry-leading technologies like VEEAM to guarantee data availability and compliance.



Key Features of Our Backup as a Service (BaaS):


• Automated Backups: We provide scheduled, automated backups that minimize the risk of data loss and reduce the burden of manual processes. Our solution supports backup for both cloud and on-prem systems, with real-time monitoring and alerts.


• Encrypted Storage: Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, ensuring that sensitive information remains secure. Our BaaS solutions meet strict industry standards for data protection, including GDPR and ISO compliance.


• Incremental Backups: Only the changes made to your data are backed up after the initial full backup, ensuring minimal storage usage while providing quick and efficient backups.


• Flexible Retention Policies: Our solutions provide customizable retention policies, allowing businesses to define how long backups are stored, ensuring both legal and regulatory compliance while optimizing storage costs.


• Rapid Data Recovery: In the event of data loss, our BaaS solution ensures fast and reliable data recovery with low RTO and RPO. Whether you need file-level recovery or full-system restoration, our service minimizes downtime and restores your business to full functionality quickly.
