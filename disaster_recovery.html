Disaster Recovery as a Service (DRaaS)

Mieux Technologies provides Disaster Recovery as a Service (DRaaS) to ensure business continuity and minimize downtime in the event of a disaster. Our DRaaS solutions offer seamless, automated failover and data recovery for critical applications, systems, and data. With our enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure, your business can recover quickly from any unforeseen disruptions, reducing risks and safeguarding your data across multi-cloud and on-premise environments.



Key Features and Benefits of DRaaS


• Fast Recovery: Achieve near-zero RTO/RPO with rapid failover and data restoration across any cloud platform, including private clouds, AWS, and Microsoft Azure.


• Data Integrity & Availability: Automatic replication of critical workloads ensures data consistency and availability, reducing the risk of data loss and enabling business continuity.


• Flexible Cloud Solutions: Choose between private cloud, hybrid cloud, or public cloud configurations for your DR needs, depending on your organization’s preferences and compliance requirements.


• Cost-Effective: Eliminate the need for expensive secondary data centers by leveraging cloud resources to store disaster recovery environments, significantly reducing infrastructure costs.


• Scalability: Scale your disaster recovery environment as needed, ensuring it grows with your business while maintaining cost-effectiveness and operational efficiency.
