Data Loss Prevention (DLP)

Mieux Technologies' Data Loss Prevention (DLP) solutions are designed to safeguard sensitive data across your organization's network, endpoints, and cloud environments. By leveraging advanced monitoring, encryption, and access control technologies, we help businesses prevent unauthorized data breaches, loss, or leaks, ensuring compliance with industry standards like GDPR, HIPAA, and others. Our DLP solutions are integrated with proactive risk management strategies, protecting your intellectual property and customer data while maintaining operational integrity.



Key Features of Data Loss Prevention


• Endpoint Protection: Our DLP tools monitor and control data across all endpoints—laptops, mobile devices, and desktops—ensuring data is encrypted, restricted, and auditable.


• Network Monitoring: We apply real-time scanning across the network to detect sensitive data in motion, preventing unauthorized access and transmission.


• Cloud Security: With our DLP services, sensitive data in the cloud is fully protected with stringent access controls and data encryption, ensuring security across platforms like AWS, Microsoft Azure, and private clouds.


• Automated Policy Enforcement: Define and enforce data handling policies automatically, preventing human error and ensuring compliance with data protection regulations.
